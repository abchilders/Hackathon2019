Shelter Intake Checklist

!!! Before completing this, please ensure caller/ youth data is filled out on opposite side!!!


CHECK the "Do Not Shelter" list at the front of the Com Log. If the youth is not on the list, proceed.

EXPLAIN: Basic YSB rules and policies. NO drug use, no violence, outing/curfew time is case by case, most stays are short. We have 24-hour staff, provide food and basic necessities, and have Internet access. We go on fun outings! Make sure to mention that YSB is NOT a lock-down facility, and that all stays are VOLUNTARY.


If you are speaking with a guardian or other adult calling for a youth, you should now inform them that you need to speak to the youth in order to complete the screening process.







If a youth is ok with us contacting their guardian, great! If they are not, it is possible that they can still be sheltered, but management must be involved in the decision. Management or emergency back up should also be involved if the youth has a history of violence or recent suicide attempt(s).
